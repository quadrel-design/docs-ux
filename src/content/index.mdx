# Welcome to Quadrel

Quadrel-UI is an integrated low-code design system.

At its core, Quadrel focuses on the rapid and intuitive creation of enterprise user interfaces by providing a rich, requirement-oriented library of components and patterns, along with an easy-to-understand API.
